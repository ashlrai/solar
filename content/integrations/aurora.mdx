Aurora is the dominant solar design tool. Ashlr complements it — we are
explicitly **not** a design tool. Aurora stays your design source of truth.

## Setup

1. In Aurora, mint an API key for your tenant.
2. In Ashlr at [/settings/integrations](https://app.ashlr.ai/settings/integrations),
   paste the key + tenant id.
3. Configure Aurora to fire `design.finalized` webhooks at
   `https://app.ashlr.ai/api/webhooks/aurora` with your Ashlr API key + HMAC signature.

See [/api/webhooks/aurora](/api/webhooks/aurora) for the wire format.

## What Ashlr does after the webhook fires

1. Pulls the full design from Aurora via your stored credentials.
2. Resolves the AHJ from the site address.
3. Runs compliance + assembles the 6-sheet permit package.
4. Persists the package + scores in your Ashlr org.
5. Sends a Slack notification (if configured) with the project URL.

## Pricing

Imported projects bill at the same per-permit rate as native ones. No
integration fee.
