title: "Ashlr vs. Greenlancer"
description: "Per-permit pricing, turnaround, AHJ coverage, predictive signals, and where each one wins."
---

Greenlancer is the incumbent permit-expediter service. You upload a design;
they run it through human PEs and permit runners, charging per-permit. Mature,
expensive, variable turnaround.

## At a glance

| Attribute | Ashlr | Greenlancer |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing | $199 / residential permit (flat) | $350–1,200 / permit |
| Turnaround | Minutes to assembled package | 5–15 business days |
| AHJ coverage | 55 codified · 30/90/180-day re-verification | US-wide (humans) |
| Approval probability | Calibrated score per project | No predictive signal |
| Plan-check response | Agent drafts in minutes, PE reviews | Human drafts, charged per revision |
| API / webhooks | Full public API, webhook intake | Email + web form |
| PE letter | Draft generated, PE stamps | Bundled (priced in) |

## When Greenlancer wins

A brand-new installer with no PE relationship needs full outsourcing. Or an
extremely unusual jurisdiction that isn't in our knowledge graph yet (we add one
a week — request via [GitHub](https://github.com/ashlrai/solar/issues/new?template=new-ahj.yml)).

## When Ashlr wins

You're processing 20+ permits/month in covered AHJs. You already have a PE.
You want predictive approval scores and fast turnaround instead of a human
queue.

## Math

At 50 permits/month:

| | Ashlr | Greenlancer |
|---|---|---|
| Per permit | $199 | $700 (midpoint) |
| Monthly | $9,950 | $35,000 |
| Annual | $119,400 | $420,000 |
| Annual savings | — | **$300,600** |

Even at the bottom of Greenlancer's range ($350/permit), Ashlr saves $90k/year
at the same volume.
